Privacy Policy

Our game uses UnityAds, a service provided by Unity Technologies, to serve ads to users. UnityAds may collect and use your data as outlined in their privacy policy.

UnityAds Privacy Policy: We recommend that you read Unity's privacy policy to understand what data they collect and how they use it. You can find their privacy policy here: Unity Privacy Policy.
